Work perfomed at UIUC

We made a new heat exchanger using polymers and metals. In the process, we created a new roll to roll (R2R) process for creating helical structures using thin (< 200 μm) materials. This HX is light, economic and and is expected to perform well in low temperature (150 ° C) flue gas conditions. We won a $1.25 million research grant from Dept. of Energy, USA to perform this project. Also refer to this list of publications related to this project.
